Specialties

Fruit Pies This category includes pies made with various fresh fruits like apple, cherry, blueberry, strawberry, peach, or mixed berries.

Cream PiesCream pies are luscious and creamy, often featuring flavors like chocolate, coconut, banana, lemon, or key lime.

Nut PiesNut pies are rich and indulgent, featuring ingredients like pecans, walnuts, almonds, or a combination of nuts. Examples include pecan pie, walnut pie, or almond caramel pie.

Custard PiesCustard pies are smooth and silky, made with a sweet and creamy filling. Popular varieties include pumpkin pie, sweet potato pie, or butterscotch pie.

Chocolate PiesChocolate lovers will enjoy this category, which includes decadent chocolate pies like chocolate silk pie, chocolate fudge pie, or triple chocolate pie.

Savory PiesThis category offers savory options like quiches or pot pies filled with ingredients such as chicken, beef, vegetables, or cheese, making them perfect for a savory pie craving.

Specialty PiesSpecialty pies encompass unique and creative flavors that are signature to the bakery. These could include flavors like salted caramel apple, s'mores pie, peanut butter cup pie, or seasonal specialties like pumpkin spice or cranberry pie.

About Us

Pie Haven Bakery, founded in 2015 by Sarah Thompson, is a testament to her lifelong love for pies. Inspired by her grandmother's cherished recipes, Sarah transformed her home into a cozy bakery, crafting pies that captured the essence of warm memories and delightful aromas. With unique flavor combinations and perfected crust-making techniques, Sarah's delectable pies quickly gained popularity. The bakery expanded into a charming storefront, featuring a café space for customers to savor pies alongside freshly brewed coffee. Today, Pie Haven remains a beloved gathering place, offering handcrafted pies made with love and the finest ingredients. Sarah's dream of sharing the simple joy of pie continues, making Pie Haven Bakery a haven for pie enthusiasts, one slice at a time.

At Pie Haven Bakery, the Art of Baking is not just a process; it's a true passion and a testament to our unwavering commitment to excellence. With every pie we create, we embrace the artistry that goes beyond mere cooking. It's about the careful selection of premium ingredients, the meticulous measurements, and the delicate balance of flavors.
